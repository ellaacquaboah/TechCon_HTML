History

TechCon was envisioned by Aaron Koomson in the year 2005. The full fledged program made a debut in 2006 after months of deliberations and negotitations with other Tech enthusiasts.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to share new ideas among people in the Tech space, enrich people's network and work towards the greater good of the greneral public.
